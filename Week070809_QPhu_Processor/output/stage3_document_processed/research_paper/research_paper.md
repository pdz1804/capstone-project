Multimodal AI in Education: A Comprehensive Study Multimodal AI in Education: A Comprehensive Study Abstract This paper presents a comprehensive analysis of multimodal artificial intelligence applications in educational technology. We investigate the integration of text, image, audio, and video processing capabilities to create enhanced learning experiences. Our methodology includes advanced document processing using state-of-the-art models for optical character recognition (OCR), natural language processing (NLP), and computer vision techniques. Keywords: Multimodal AI, Educational Technology, Document Processing, OCR, NLP, Computer Vision

1. Introduction The integration of multimodal AI systems in education represents a significant advancement in how we process and understand educational content. Traditional methods of content analysis were limited to single modalities, but modern approaches can simultaneously process text, images, audio, and structured data.
