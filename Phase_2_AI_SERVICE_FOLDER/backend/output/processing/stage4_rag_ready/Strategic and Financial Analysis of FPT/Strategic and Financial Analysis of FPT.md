## Strategic and Financial Analysis of FPT Corporation: AI-First Transition, Competitive Positioning, and Global Expansion as of Q1 2026

## 1 Executive Summary

FPT Corporation enters Q1 2026 as a financially robust, strategically well-positioned IT services company whose 'AI-First' transformation is producing measurable results, though its path to competing with global majors still faces meaningful scale constraints. FY2025 delivered VND 70.1 trillion in revenue (+11.6% YoY) and VND 11.2 trillion in profit after tax (+19.1%), with profit growth significantly outpacing revenue growth - evidence that the shift toward higher-margin AI and digital transformation services is driving genuine margin expansion rather than volume-dependent earnings [1].

The AI-First transition is the most consequential strategic development. FPT's three-layered FleziPT platform, $200 million investment in NVIDIA-powered AI factories, and IvyHub agentic AI suite have earned substantive third-party validation, including IDC MarketScape Leader status in conversational AI and multiple Everest Group Major Contender recognitions [2, 3, 4]. The strategy of converting 25,000 engineers into AI-augmented workers positions FPT to deliver productivity-driven margin gains while offering cost-competitive AI services from its Vietnamese talent base [5].

Global expansion is accelerating but remains concentrated. International IT revenue reached approximately $1.44 billion with newly signed contracts exceeding $1.5 billion (+23.2%), and FPT climbed to 40th among Asian IT services firms [6, 7]. Japan dominates as the anchor market, on track toward a $1 billion revenue target by 2027 [8], while landmark deals in the US, Germany, Singapore, and Indonesia demonstrate broadening geographic reach [31-34]. However, FPT's entire international revenue remains roughly 2% of Accenture's, limiting competitiveness for the largest global engagements [9]. Revenue growth deceleration from 19.4% to 11.6% also warrants monitoring as the base scales [10].

The critical actionable insight is that FPT's convergence of AI-first capabilities, a deepening enterprise partnership ecosystem (ServiceNow Premier Partner, NVIDIA, Microsoft), and cost-advantaged delivery model positions it to capture disproportionate share of the mid-market and upper-mid-market AI transformation wave - the segment where its scale disadvantage matters least and its growth premium over incumbents is most pronounced.

## 2 Introduction

FPT Corporation (HOSE: FPT), Vietnam's largest technology conglomerate, stands at a strategic inflection point as it enters 2026. Having delivered robust financial results in FY2025 - with revenue surpassing VND 70 trillion and profit after tax growing nearly 20% year-over-year - the company is aggressively pursuing an 'AI-First' transformation while scaling its international operations across Japan, the United States, Europe, and emerging markets [1, 7]. FPT now ranks 40th among Asia's IT services companies and 139th globally, generating over $1.4 billion in international technology revenue [6]. This report provides a comprehensive evaluation of FPT's financial health, AI-first strategy execution, competitive positioning relative to global IT majors, and the effectiveness of its geographic diversification as of Q1 2026. With Q1 2026 results expected on April 21, 2026 [1], the analysis draws on FY2025 full-year data, analyst forecasts, and the latest strategic developments through March 2026.

## 3 Financial Performance and Outlook

FY2025 Results: Profitable Growth with Margin Expansion. FPT reported full-year 2025 revenue of VND 70,113 billion, representing 11.6% year-over-year growth, while profit after tax reached VND 11,226 billion, expanding 19.1% over the prior year [1]. Pre-tax profit climbed to VND 13,039 billion [7]. The company's gross profit of VND 25,895 billion translates to a gross margin of approximately 36.9%, with a net profit margin of roughly 16.0% and a pre-tax margin of about 18.6% [1]. Earnings per share rose 21.4% to VND 7,857, meaningfully outpacing revenue growth and signaling genuine margin expansion rather than top-line-driven earnings [1].

The quality of FPT's earnings is underscored by the fact that profit growth (~19% PAT) significantly exceeded revenue growth (~11.6%), driven by the high-margin Technology sector, which contributed 61% of group revenue and 47% of pre-tax profit [11]. The company's cash position also strengthened, with cash and cash equivalents rising to VND 10,540 billion at year-end 2025, up from VND 9,315 billion at end-2024 [1]. This liquidity buffer provides strategic flexibility for continued M&amp;A activity and AI infrastructure investments.

Growth Trajectory and 2026 Outlook. It is worth noting that FPT's revenue growth decelerated from approximately 19.4% in FY2024 to 11.6% in FY2025, though profitability growth remained robust [10]. In FY2024, FPT had recorded revenue of VND 62,849 billion with pre-tax profit growth of 20.3% [10]. Looking ahead, analysts forecast FPT's earnings and revenue growth at approximately 15.7% and 12.6% per annum respectively, with EPS expected to grow by about 14.5% per annum [12]. Broader market sentiment in Vietnam is also supportive, with many Vietnamese corporates setting 20-30% full-year profit growth targets for 2026 [13].

## 4 AI-First Transformation: FleziPT and the Agentic AI Stack

The FleziPT Platform Architecture. At the core of FPT's AI-first strategy is FleziPT, a comprehensive platform structured across three interconnected layers. The Flezi Foundation layer drives internal transformation through an AI-driven software development lifecycle (SDLC), incorporating Responsible AI Governance and co-innovation partnerships with NVIDIA, Microsoft, SAP, and Landing AI. The Flezi Workforce layer is transforming FPT's traditional engineering base into 25,000 AI-Augmented Engineers while deploying Digital Workers to automate routine tasks. The Flezi Verse layer delivers customer-facing AI offerings, including the IQ Data Platform and Flezi Agents [5]. This three-tier approach ensures that AI permeates not only FPT's client deliverables but also its internal operations and talent development.

IvyHub and IvyChat: Enterprise Agentic AI. FPT's IvyHub platform serves as a unified agentic AI solution - a one-stop shop featuring a low-code/no-code interface that enables enterprises to create custom AI models and manage multiagent processes spanning conversational AI and workflow automation. IvyChat, a key product within IvyHub, provides comprehensive AI-powered conversational solutions for enterprises, including chatbots, virtual assistants, and voice agents [2]. The platform's emphasis on accessibility through low-code interfaces positions FPT to capture demand from enterprises seeking rapid AI deployment without deep technical expertise.

NVIDIA Partnership and AI Factory Infrastructure. FPT has invested $200 million to establish AI factories powered by NVIDIA H100 Tensor Core GPUs and NVIDIA AI Enterprise software, creating a one-stop shop for AI and cloud services across Vietnam, Japan, and other markets [14]. In partnership with NVIDIA and DDN, FPT has developed two advanced AI factories in Vietnam and Japan, delivering high-performance computing capabilities and end-to-end AI lifecycle services [15]. The broader AI Factory ecosystem includes partnerships with SCSK, ASUS, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, VAST Data, and DDN Storage [16]. In 2025, the FPT AI Factory platform successfully launched 43 AI services covering the full AI lifecycle, from data processing and model training to deployment, inference, and monitoring [17].

Industry Analyst Recognition. FPT's AI capabilities have earned significant third-party validation. FPT Software was positioned as a Leader in the IDC MarketScape: Asia/Pacific AI-Enabled Front Office Conversational AI Software 2025 Vendor Assessment, which evaluated 17 vendors and recognized FPT for its end-to-end offering leveraging NVIDIA's high-performance computing stack [2]. Additionally, FPT achieved Major Contender status in at least three Everest Group PEAK Matrix 2025 assessments: Application Development Services for AI Applications (evaluated among 35 providers) [3], Banking IT Services (its first appearance in this benchmark, among 41 providers) [4], and Digital Transformation Services for Mid-market Enterprises [18]. FPT was also recognized among 14 Representative Vendors in the Gartner Market Guide for Microsoft 365 Implementation and Support Services 2026 [19].

## 6 Competitive Positioning in Global IT Services

FPT operates in a global IT services market dominated by significantly larger players. While FPT's international IT revenue of approximately $1.44 billion [7] is a fraction of the revenues generated by Accenture (~$65 billion) [9], TCS ($31 billion) [24], Infosys ($20 billion) [24], or Wipro (~$11 billion) [25], FPT's growth trajectory and AI-first positioning create meaningful competitive differentiation.
